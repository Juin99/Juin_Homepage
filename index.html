About Me

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Miao Lu. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Probability and Statistics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2022. I was fortunate to be enrolled in the School of the Gifted Young at USTC, for which I skipped my third year of high school. In the year of 2022, I won the Guo Moruo scholarship, the highest honor awarding undergraduates in USTC. Starting from 2023 Fall, I am joining the Department of Management Science and Engineering at Stanford University as a Ph.D. student.

Research Interests

I have broad interests in the span of machine learning, statistics, and optimization. I enjoy using mathematical and computational tools to develop theoretical foundations and principled methods for real-world problems. If you are interested in my background or want to chat or collaborate, feel free to contact me.
